Online Maps as Storytelling Devices

  • Cyberspace is big. Maps ground your content.
  • Engage local communities.
  • Show the breadth of movements.
  • Make stuff "far away" feel more accessible.
  • Visualize change over time.
  • Drill down into complex datasets.

Even the most basic maps wield influence because of the historical perception that they are hard to create. Leverage this fact for your cause.

What Makes an Online Map Feel Definitive?

  • Uniqueness of the map tiles (i.e., not Google Maps)
  • Uniqueness of iconography
  • Colors, colors, colors - Convey values
  • Presence of a legend - Conveys authority
  • Interactivity - Conveys timeliness of data
  • Multimedia integration - Personalizes map data
  • Seamless integration of map in your website - Suggests map is canonical
